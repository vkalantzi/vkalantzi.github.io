About


I am a Manager and Senior Research Scientist at IBM Research. Prior to my current role I was a Herman H. Goldstine Memorial Postdoctoral Fellow. I received my PhD (2018) and M.Sc (2016) in Computer Science and Engineering from the University of Minnesota, Twin Cities, U.S., and M.Sc (2014) and M.Eng (2011) in Computer Engineering and Informatics from the University of Patras, Greece.




PhD Thesis


I obtained my PhD (Advisor: Yousef Saad) in 09/2018 from the University of Minnesota, Twin Cities. My dissertation is titled "Domain decomposition algorithms for the solution of sparse symmetric generalized eigenvalue problems". You can view/download a copy here.
